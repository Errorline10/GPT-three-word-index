FundingTrustClarity

Founder-led, community-built, and transparent about how money and ownership work.

Three Word Index is currently a founder-funded, volunteer-supported open-source project. Contributions do not automatically create employment, equity, ownership, or revenue-sharing rights.

If the hosted project generates revenue, funds first support infrastructure, security, compliance, moderation, and a responsible operating reserve. Contributor bounties or paid work may follow when resources permit.

CommonProjectQuestions

Is this supposed to make bots impossible?

No. The goal is to increase the cost and inconvenience of bulk automated extraction while keeping normal human use practical.

Do I need to understand the entire architecture?

No. You can contribute documentation, design, tests, infrastructure, or take ownership of one small content-block mini-project.

Is this cryptocurrency?

No. The proposed coin and display-token systems are internal platform accounting mechanisms, not speculative cryptocurrency.

Are contributors paid?

Contributions are currently voluntary. Future funding may support bounties, contracts, grants, or maintainer work, but no payment or employment is promised.

Can students and newer developers contribute?

Yes. The project intends to maintain small issues, documentation tasks, testing work, and self-contained component projects for different experience levels.
